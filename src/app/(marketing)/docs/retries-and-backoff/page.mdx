# Retries & backoff

When a delivery fails, Odyhook retries it on a fixed exponential schedule. There are 6 attempts in total — 1 initial attempt plus 5 retries. If every attempt is exhausted, the delivery stays visible in the dashboard for one-click replay.

The backoff schedule — the delay applied before the next attempt — is:

| Step | Delay |
|---|---|
| 1 | 10s |
| 2 | 30s |
| 3 | 2m |
| 4 | 10m |
| 5 | 1h |
| 6 | 6h |

Terminal (non-retryable) outcomes stop early instead of exhausting all attempts — there's no point retrying a delivery that can never succeed.
