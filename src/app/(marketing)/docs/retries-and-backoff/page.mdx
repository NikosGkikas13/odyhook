# Retries & backoff

When a delivery fails, Odyhook retries it on a fixed exponential schedule. There are up to 6 attempts in total — 1 initial attempt plus 5 retries. If every attempt is exhausted, the delivery stays visible in the dashboard for one-click replay.

The initial attempt runs immediately. Each retry then waits a growing delay after the previous failure:

| Retry | Delay after the previous attempt |
|---|---|
| 1st | 30s |
| 2nd | 2m |
| 3rd | 10m |
| 4th | 1h |
| 5th | 6h |

After the 5th retry fails, the delivery is marked exhausted.

Terminal (non-retryable) outcomes stop early instead of exhausting all attempts — there's no point retrying a delivery that can never succeed.
